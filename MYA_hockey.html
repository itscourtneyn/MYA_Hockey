Welcome to MYA Hockey.

Who we are:


The McDonough Youth Inline Hockey Association (MYIHA) is a Henry County, Georgia hockey club in which every child who is a member can learn to skate and play the game of hockey. Our organization's objective is to teach skating skills and the fundamentals of the game, team effort, and good sportsmanship.
